ABOUT ANUGRAHA

One Of The Largest Textile Manufacturers In India

We are Anugraha Fashion, a high quality vertically integrated garment manufacturer based in the knitwear capital of South India – Tirupur. Incorporated in 1988 and our core skills are manufacturing & exporting of knitted garments.

Our manufacturing process has been vertically integrated to facilitate all the processes involved in Garment Manufacture under one roof. We have our manufacturing facilities spread in four locations – consisting of Spinning, Knitting, Processing and Garmenting divisions, making us a totally integrated manufacturer from fibre to fashion garment…

Sustainability

Good Health & Well-Being

We strive to continuously improve our commitment towards Occupational Health & Safety Standards through improved & sustainable technology, whereby we reduce the risks and prevent accidents in the workplace.

Employee Welfare

Workplace is designed to make employees feel at home. We provide them with opportunities to learn and grow, Free Transport facilities, Nutritious food,& separate hostel facilities for women and men.

Women Empowerment

Women employees contribute more than 50% in our entire workforce. Women Empowerment projects at regular intervals ensure their rights and responsibilities, skill enhancement and financial independency.

Environmental Sustainability

We believe in responsible business growth by investing in resource conservation and efficiency across water, energy, chemicals and waste management.
